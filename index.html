Welcome to Your PCS Planning Tool

Moving with the military can feel overwhelming, but with the right preparation and organization, you can navigate the process smoothly. This interactive checklist is designed to help you stay on track throughout every phase of your accompanied CONUS to CONUS permanent change of station (PCS).

Whether you're managing a government-procured move (GPM), handling a personally procured move (PPM), or somewhere in between, this comprehensive guide breaks down the entire process into manageable steps organized by timeline and phase.

What You'll Find Inside

  • Eight organized sections covering every stage of your move, from receiving orders to settling into your new duty station
  • Real-time progress tracking with overall and section-specific progress bars
  • Automatic progress saving that persists when you close and reopen the checklist
  • Mobile-friendly design so you can access it anywhere, anytime
  • Links to official resources including DPS, Military One Source, TRICARE, and more
  • Expert tips and critical notes to help you avoid common mistakes and understand key regulations

How to Use This Checklist

Simply click on any item or checkbox to mark it complete. Your progress is saved automatically to your browser, so you can close and return to this checklist anytime. The progress bar at the top shows your overall completion percentage, while each section header displays that section's progress separately.

When all items in a section are complete, the section header will turn green. Use the "Reset All" button if you need to start over completely.

What This Checklist Covers

Section I: First Steps After Receiving Orders — Get organized and submit your housing application immediately. Learn about creating a move binder and understanding key documents.

Section II: 180–90 Days Before Moving — Understand your weight allowance, decide between GPM and PPM options, schedule shipments in DPS, plan transportation, and address important administrative tasks like ID renewals.

Section III: 90–30 Days Before Moving — Plan your pet's relocation, collect medical records, contact school liaisons, and explore childcare options at your new duty station.

Section IV: Final 30 Days — Notify financial institutions, submit address changes, finalize utilities, and prepare your household for packing and move-out.

Section V: Travel to Your New PDS — Keep receipts and track weather along your route during transit.

Section VI: After Arriving — Check in with your unit, update TRICARE, finalize housing, complete travel vouchers, and verify entitlement payments.

Section VII: Government Procured Move (GPM) Guide — Detailed instructions for TSP coordination, moving day procedures, acceptance of shipments, and filing claims if needed.

Section VIII: Personally Procured Move (PPM) Guide — Everything you need to manage your own move, including weight tickets, expense documentation, and voucher submission.

Important Notes

This checklist references Department of Transportation Regulations (DTR) and Joint Travel Regulations (JTR) that may vary by service branch. While this tool is designed for all military branches, be sure to verify specific requirements with your local Personal Property Office (PPO) and finance office, as procedures and entitlements may differ.

All information is current as of May 2026. Always consult official resources and your chain of command for the most up-to-date guidance.
