Welcome to Your PCS Planning Tool

Moving with the military can feel overwhelming, but with the right preparation and organization, you can navigate the process smoothly. This interactive checklist is designed to help you stay on track throughout every phase of your accompanied permanent change of station (PCS).

Whether you're managing a move within CONUS, relocating to Hawaii, Guam, Japan, or returning from overseas, this comprehensive guide breaks down the entire process into manageable steps organized by timeline and phase.

What You'll Find Inside

Comprehensive checklists covering every stage of your move, from receiving orders to settling into your new duty station

Real-time progress tracking with overall progress indicators

Automatic progress saving that persists when you close and reopen the checklist

Mobile-friendly design so you can access it anywhere, anytime

Links to official resources including JTR, DTR, Personal Property Activity, and MARADMINS

Expert tips and critical notes to help you avoid common mistakes and understand key regulations
